Rub garlic on your nails to make them stronger.

Steps:

1. Take one small garlic clove and chop into fine pieces until minced

2. Add the minced garlic to a bottle of clear nail polish and let it sit for 5-10 days.

3. After the garlic has been sitting for a couple of days, paint your nails with the polish.

4. To remove odor, wash your hands with fresh squeezed lemon juice.

Use hairspray and a wet cloth to get out lipstick stains.

Steps:

1. Spray the hairspray onto the lipstick stain.

2. Allow the hairspray to sit on the lipstick stain for up to 15 minutes.

3. Take a clean cloth and dip in warm water.

4. Wipe the hairspray off the stain with the cloth.

5. Wash the fabric as usual.
