Clothing Hacks

Widen shoes by filling them with water.

Window cleaner will also clean patent leather.

Use vaseline to unstick a zipper.

Put your jeans in the freezer to make them smell less.

Wash sneakers with baking soda, detergent and a toothbrush to liven them up.

>

Hang shirts quickly by sticking your hand through them one by one.

If you stain your clothes with red wine, use white wine to remove it.

To solve your peeping pants problem, follow these steps:

1. Attach a small key ring to your zipper.
2. Put your pants on, and zip up the lazy zipper, looping the ring around the button.
​3. Fasten the button, concealing the ring.
