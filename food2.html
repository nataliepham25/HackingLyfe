Food Hacks

The Perfect Ice Cream Sandwhich!

All you need is an ice cream carton and two cookies. At the tip of the ice cream carton cut it off so that you get a circular piece of ice cream and place it on the middle of the two cookies.

Pancake Pops

The next time you make pancakes make small round pancakes, add a stick to the batter and let it cook with the stick in it.

Grill Fish On A Bed Of Lemons

Place the fillet on a bed of lemons and you won’t be scraping salmon off of the grates.

Store Natural Nut Butters Upside Down

Store natural nut butters upside down to make it easier to stir and then spread…and then eat large quantities of.

Bread Slices Keep Old Cake Moist

After cutting into a cake, use toothpicks to cover the exposed portion with piece of bread to keep cake nice and soft.

How to get boiled eggs to peel easily

1. Add one teaspoon of baking soda when you boil your eggs.

2. It’ll make the shell come off effortlessly.

Ensure your iced coffee and iced cappuccino doesn’t get watered down

1. Pour coffee into ice cube holders

2. The next time you have a iced coffee or cappuccino pop those coffee cubes into your drink
