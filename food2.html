Food Hacks

Use a straw to de-stem a strawberry

1. First get a bunch of strawberries

2. Get one straw; any color

3. Push the straw all the way through the tip of the strawberry

4. TAH-DAH the green leaf comes right off!

Make sure you are drinking enough water throughout the day

1. Get a tall water bottle (try to make sure that it is clear)

2. Get a variety of colored sharpies

3. Draw multiple lines around the entire water bottle and make sure the lines are evenly spaced

4. Above each line write a time of day you would like to finish your water at

5. For Example: @12pm you're halfway through the water bottle

Keep your popsicle from dripping!

1. You will need a cupcake wrapper

2. A popsicle (any kind)

3. Take the cupcake wrapper and make a hole in the middle of it. Big enough for the popsicle stick to go through

4. Then put the popsicle stick through the whole so that the cupcake wrapper cups the bottom of the popsicle

Make limp celery crisp again!

1. Cut the celery into sticks. This means removing the base and the leaf ends.

2. Place the cut sticks into a bowl of iced water. Leave for half an hour.

3. The sticks should be revived and as good as new!

How to get boiled eggs to peel easily

1. Add one teaspoon of baking soda when you boil your eggs.

2. It’ll make the shell come off effortlessly.

Ensure your iced coffee and iced cappuccino doesn’t get watered down

1. Pour coffee into ice cube holders

2. The next time you have a iced coffee or cappuccino pop those coffee cubes into your drink
