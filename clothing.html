Clothing Hacks

De-Pill Your Clothing Using A Razor

1. You get a razor and run it over your clothing. After you do this get some tape to pick any left overs

Wrinkly Shirt? No Problem!

Throw any wrinkly shirt or clothing into a dryer for 5 minutes with ice cubes

Use a Hair Straightener As a Collar Iron

Just get any hair sraightener and iron your collar with it. Don’t have the heat to high or leave the straightener on the fabric too long or you may cause damage… and a fire.

Use Shaving Foam To Remove Make-Up Stains

Get some shaving foam and pray some on any makeup stain you have on a piece of clothing and it will take it right out

Try Jeans On Without Actually Trying Them On

The next time you're at a store shopping for jeans warp the waist of jeans around your neck and if the tips of the pair of jeans touch perfectly then they fit.

Pull On The Fabric Around A Thread To Pull It Back Into Place

As you can see from the picture to the left, all you really have to do is pull around the area where the thread is.

Cover Sneakers In Baking Soda To Remove Odours

Basically just sprinkle baking soda all over your shoes. The baking soda will soak up any sweat that’s on your shoes. Gross, but true.

Use A Sharpie To Cover Bleach Stains

Bleach stains are the worst. There's no two ways about it. Whoever says they aren't that bad simply doesn't know what they're talking about. Color over them with a Sharpie to make them disappear!
